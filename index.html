Our Clinical Research Expertise

Clinical Trial Management

Regulatory Support

CBSE Consultancy Services

Expert guidance for CBSE school affiliations and compliance

Your Partner in Educational Excellence

Innoverritas provides comprehensive CBSE consultancy services to help educational institutions navigate the complex process of obtaining and maintaining CBSE affiliation. Led by Ajay Singh Rajput, our team brings in-depth knowledge of CBSE requirements and regulations.

Our Services

New Affiliation Support

Complete guidance for schools seeking CBSE affiliation, from initial application to final approval.

Compliance Consulting

Ensuring ongoing compliance with CBSE norms, regulations, and quality standards.

Documentation Support

Expert assistance with all required documentation, forms, and submissions to CBSE.

Infrastructure Assessment

Evaluation and recommendations for meeting CBSE infrastructure and facility requirements.

The Affiliation Process

Obtaining CBSE affiliation requires meticulous attention to detail and thorough understanding of CBSE guidelines. We guide you through every step:

  • Initial assessment and eligibility verification
  • Infrastructure and facility evaluation
  • Documentation preparation and submission
  • CBSE inspection preparation and support
  • Post-affiliation compliance and renewal assistance

Why Choose Innoverritas?

Our deep understanding of CBSE requirements, combined with our commitment to excellence, ensures that your institution receives the expert guidance needed for successful affiliation. We provide personalized support tailored to your institution's unique needs and circumstances.
